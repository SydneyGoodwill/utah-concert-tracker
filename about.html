About

This is a simple Concert-tracking website, meant to help the good people of Northern-ish Utah find out about concerts and events going on in their area. While by no means a perfect solution, this simple site narrows down the search by including a list of venues across Salt Lake City, Ogden, and beyond. You can search a venue via the dropdown menu, or by typing in a venue and searching through the options that way. I've also included a map of all of the venues on this site, so finding the address should be pretty easy.

The user can also keep notes or make tentative plans, by using the note-taker on the Plans/Notes page.

As a work in progress, this little tracker is always open to feedback, suggestions, and always happy to add new venues to the list! To reach out, please visit the Contact page. Thank you for using the site!
